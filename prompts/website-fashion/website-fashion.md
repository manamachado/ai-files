Visual Strategy:
Imagery: natural textures(linen, organic cotton), handcrafted details, minimal tailoring.
Photography: shot in natural light, real-world settings(forest, seaside, grassland).
Viewing Angles: highlight material texture and craftsmanship details.

Color Palette:
Primary Colors: earth tones(warm ivory, light brown, olive green).
Accent Colors: deep green, terracotta red.
Background: solid color or a subtle paper texture.

Typography:
Headings: a humanist serif.
Body Text: comfortable line spacing, with magazine-like editorial layout.
leave generous whitespace around text and images.

Page Structure:
Hero Section: brand manifesto + large-format environmental photography.
Product Showcase: organized by scenarios(home, commuting, outdoor).
Sustainability Commitment: material details, transparent production process.
Community Stories: real customers sharing outfits and lifestyle moments.

Overall Atmosphere: natural, warm, responsible, design-led.
